Cup Magnifier

 

By using plastic cups and water as a simple lense, you can  make a basic magnifier.

Materials:  

  • 2 Pint Plastic cups
  • Cling Film
  • Rubber Bands
  • Water
  • Leaf
  • Shot glass

Tools:

  • Craft Scissors

Instructions

  1. Take one cup and cut the (rim)top off. This should be approximately 5 cm in depth. Discard the bottom part of the cup in the bin provided.
  2. Place a piece of cling film on this rim and secure with an elastic band. Now turn the rim upside down, so the top is now the bottom.
  3. Put a leaf into the second cup and then carefully balance the first cup on top. The cling film side should sit on the bottom.
  4. Finally add  water to the top of the cling film.
  5. Now look at the leaf through the water lense you have made.

Observations and Questions

  1. Does the leaf look bigger or smaller?
  2. What challenges do you think a water lense might give a scientist?
  3. Why do they use glass lenses?
